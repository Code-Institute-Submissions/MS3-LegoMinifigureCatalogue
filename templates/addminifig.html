To add your own minifigure and expand the catalogue, just fill out the form below.

When taking a photo of your minifigure, remember the following:

  • Take the photo in potrait, not landscape
  • Take the photo in good light
  • Make sure your minifig is in the centre of the photo
  • Make sure your minifig has all the accessories included
